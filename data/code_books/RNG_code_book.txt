Code book of of RNG_original.csv

Original article: “Villani et al (2025) Open hands, large numbers.pdf”

Variables:

subject: a unique identifier for participants

language`: what language the participant spoke (categorical: German vs. Norwegian)

movement`: the nature of the gesture accompanying the random number generation (categorical: outwards, inwards)

number: the number generated (numeric, 1-30)

trial: the trial number, i.e. which position in the experimental sequence (numeric, 1-110)
