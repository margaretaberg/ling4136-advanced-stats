Code book of of snarc_nuerk05.csv

Original article: “Nuerk et al (2005). The Universal SNARC Effect.pdf”

Variables:

subject: a unique participant identity

trial.no: trial number, i.e. which observation in the experiment (1-200)

number: one of 10 stimuli. Numbers 0-9.

hand: the hand with which the correct answer had to be given (right or left)

RT: reaction time in milliseconds

correct: whether the response was correct (1) or not (0)
