Code book of of quantese_naming_sim.csv

These data are simulated and loosely based on the literature on L2 picture naming tasks.

Variables:

age_yrs: participant’s age in years at time of testing (numerical)

age_of_onset: participant’s age in years when they started learning Quantese (numerical)

Condition: whether the word was Simple or Complex (categorical)

log_frequency: the log-transformed frequency count of the word (numerical)

ACC: whether response was accurate (1) or inaccurate (0) (binary)

rt: the reaction time in milliseconds (numerical)

PartID: the participant ID number

ItemID: the item ID number
