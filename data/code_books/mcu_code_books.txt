Code book of of mcu_box_office_class.csv

Dataset curated from sources online on ratings, revenue and costs of mcu movies.

Variables:

movie_title: movie title
mcu_phase: which phase does the movie belong to according to marvel (1-5)
relase_date: cinematic release
tomato_meter: tomato_meter score from rottentomatoes.com
audience_score: popcornmeter from rottentomatoes.com
movie_duration: duration of movie in minutes
production_budget: production budget in US $
opening_weekend: box office revenue at opening weekend in US $ corrected for inflation in 2023.
worldwide_box_office: gross box office revenue worldwide in US $ corrected for inflation in 2023.
